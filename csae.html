Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Terra is committed to preventing and combating child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). These standards describe how we address these harms and keep our community safe.

Prohibited content and conduct

We do not allow any content or behavior that sexualizes or exploits minors. This includes, but is not limited to: child sexual abuse material (CSAM), content that promotes or facilitates child sexual exploitation, grooming, or any representation of minors in a sexual or suggestive context.

Detection and prevention

We use a combination of human review and technical measures to detect, remove, and report harmful content and activity. We take action on violating content and accounts in line with our policies and applicable law.

Reporting

If you see content or behavior that you believe exploits or endangers a child, please report it in the app or contact us via our Support page. We treat reports seriously and act in accordance with our policies and legal obligations.

Cooperation with authorities

We cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities in the investigation of child sexual abuse and exploitation, including where required by law.

These standards are part of our commitment to a safe environment for everyone. We may update this page from time to time; the current version is always published here.
